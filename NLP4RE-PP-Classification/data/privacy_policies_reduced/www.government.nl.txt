Policy: 
Reduced Policy:   You can submit a request in any of the following situations, if you wish to:  know which personal data of yours the ministry is processing;amend your personal data;have your personal data deleted;limit the processing of your personal data;lodge an objection.  You can submit your request to the Government of the Netherlands by emailingavg@minaz.nl. Alternatively, you can send your request to the following address:  Ministry of General AffairsFunctionaris PersoonsgegevensPostbus 200012500 EA The HagueThe Netherlands
